Marcin Szocinski was born in Poland where he explored different art forms including film and theater. At the age of 17 he migrated to Chicago and soon after attended university, where he studied cinematography. Over the years, his work in the film/TV industry has enabled Marcin to travel extensively and experience many diverse productions. He immersed himself in an array of many cultures working with international directors and crews. This unique experience has shaped his special visual language. He is currently based in Los Angeles.
